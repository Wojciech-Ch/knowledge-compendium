ngOnInit

Called once, after creating the component and initializing its input properties. An ideal place for business logic initialization.

ngOnChanges

Called when one or more input properties of the component change. Provides a SimpleChanges object.

ngDoCheck

Called with each change detection run, even those not automatically detected. Allows for manual change detection.

ngAfterContentInit

Called once after placing external content in the component's view.

ngAfterContentChecked

Called after every check of the component's external content.

ngAfterViewInit

Called once after initializing the component's view and child views. A safe place to manipulate DOM.

ngAfterViewChecked

Called after every check of the component's view and child views.

ngOnDestroy

Called just before destroying the component. A place for cleanup activities, like unsubscribing.
